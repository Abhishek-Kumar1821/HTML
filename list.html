OUR ENVIRONMENT

Every living and non living things around us make our Environment. Our environment is the surroundings in which we live, including the air, water, land, plants, and animals. It provides us with the necessary resources for survival and supports all life on Earth. The environment is essential for our well-being and plays a crucial role in maintaining the balance of nature.
